The Wash Center
Enterprise AI power with zero data exposure

The Challenge

The most powerful AI models need massive computing power you don't have in your office. But sending client data to the cloud? Unacceptable. The Wash Center solves this.

How It Works

1. Analyze Locally
Your system processes documents on your hardware. Originals never move.
2. Strip Identifiers
"John Smith at 123 Main St" becomes "Party_A at Location_1". Meaning preserved, identity gone.
3. Send Anonymous Patterns
Only language patterns, tone markers, timeline structures go out. Encrypted in transit.
4. Heavy Processing
Wash Center runs 70B+ parameter models, compares against millions of cases.
5. Insights Return
"Pattern matches deception signature with 87% confidence" - findings, not data.
6. Re-Connect Locally
Your system maps insights back to real names. Wash Center never knew who was who.
